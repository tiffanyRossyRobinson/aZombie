***aZombie: Zombie News Alerts***

aZombie is a site created to provide alerts and updates on potential Zombie outbreaks. In addition, aZombie also provides survival tips to help you survive in the event of an outbreak. Other related news also provided.

Outreak News

Find out where current outbreaks have occured! Plus, find out what destinations are NOT recommended for travel due to scientific and/or military activity in the area that could result in a zombie outbreak!!!

Science Today

Find current news for scientific research being done that could result in outbreak. Also, find other relevant knews.

Survival Tips

Survive that outbreak! Find tools, tips, and rules needed to help you survive the outbreak! Use your brain, don't lose your brain!

Test Survival Skills

With this interactive simulation of an outbreak, test and practice your survival skills. Run away from zombies, hunt for supplies, and save your family!

Are You Ready?

So you think you are prepared for a zombie apocalypse? Let us be the judge. Take the aZombie Are You Ready? quiz to find out how long you would actually last in the event of an outbreak.

eNews Sign Up

Don't be the last to know! Sign up for the aZombie eNews letter and receive updates through your email. Your time is precious! Getting these updates might just save your life!
